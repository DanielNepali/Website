Welcome to you all in this page

I want to thank you all for taking your time to visit this page to know about us. We would like to share what we are all about and what we do.

We do many fun events such as trekking, outreach, bible study in the woods, picnics, rafting, etc..

Life is not just about stress, work and study. But we should have fun in life. And even with God we should be able to enjoy and have a fun time to worship him through our deeds.
