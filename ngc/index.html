Inter Church Soccer Tournament

We organize the Nayagoan Soccer competition every year. This is a way to gather youths from different churches. They participate to play soccer to glorify God with their game. This is also done to raise support for the youth ministry. Through this sport, gospel have been able to reach to many youth faster.
